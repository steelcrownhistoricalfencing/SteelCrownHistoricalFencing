# Godinho Montante

[← Back to Materials](../Materials.md){ .md-button }

*Author: Chris Mineweaser*

An interpretation of the Montante section from Domingo Luis Godinho's *Arte de Esgrima* (1599). Covers Godinho's fourteen rules for the great two-handed sword, simply presented.

---

## What's Inside

**Introduction** — Overview of the Montante and its role in Godinho's system.

**Glossary** — Key terms used throughout the text.

**Rules 1–14** — Godinho's rules for the Montante, covering scenarios from narrow streets and wide streets to guarding persons and goods, separating fights, and the master's flourish.
